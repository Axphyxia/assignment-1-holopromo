Motoaki Tanigo, President and CEO

After graduating from the Faculty of Science and Technology of Keio University,
he was put in charge of game development in cooperation with Sanrio Co. Ltd. at
Imagineer Co. Ltd. overseeing the operation of the official mobile website in
partnership with TV stations and publishers. After participating in the launch of
EC business at iStyle Co. Ltd. which is operated by the cosmetics word-of-mouth
site @cosme, and the founding of mobile advertising company Interspire Co. Ltd.
(currently United) and then founded Sun Zero Minute Co. Ltd. and developed
O2O business centered smartphone app which is Japan's first GPS compatible
app that allows Restaurant curation. Making that as the main focus of said app
"30min" pronounced as Sun zero minute and sold it to UNITED Co. Ltd. and
founded a new company focusing on AR and VR Technology in 2016. Has a
nickname of "Yagoo" that orginated from one of his talent's live stream where
his name of Tanigo was read wrongly.
